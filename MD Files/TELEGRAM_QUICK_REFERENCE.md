## Quick Decision Guide

### ✅ APPROVED (Core Setup)
**Conditions:**
- Confidence ≥ 70%
- Entry Quality ≥ 70
- Trend Alignment ≥ 70%
- Regime Score ≥ 70

**Action:** Safe to trade

---

### 🧪 APPROVED (Experimental)
**Conditions:**
- Confidence ≥ 50%
- Entry Quality ≥ 40
- Research mode ON

**Action:** Trade if comfortable with risk

---

### ❌ BLOCKED
**Conditions:**
- Any score too low
- Risk limit exceeded
- Position limit reached

**Action:** Wait for better conditions

---

## Risk Warnings

| Warning | Meaning | Action |
|---------|---------|--------|
| ⚠️ Chart Conflict | 1H vs 3H disagree | Reduce size or wait |
| ⚠️ Position Limit | Already at max trades | Close existing trade |
| ⚠️ Daily Loss | Approaching loss limit | Reduce size or stop |

---

## What To Do

### IF APPROVED ✅
1. Review setup on your chart
2. Confirm entry/exit levels
3. Place trade if you agree

### IF EXPERIMENTAL 🧪
1. Understand the risk
2. Reduce position size
3. Trade only if comfortable

### IF BLOCKED ❌
1. Wait for next scan
2. Monitor for better setup
3. Close existing trades if needed

---

## Trading Instructions

### FOR BULLISH TRADES 🟢
- **Entry:** Buy Call (CE) at ATM or slightly OTM
- **Stop Loss:** Below support level
- **Target:** Resistance level
- **Exit:** Trail SL or take profit at target

### FOR BEARISH TRADES 🔴
- **Entry:** Buy Put (PE) at ATM or slightly OTM
- **Stop Loss:** Above resistance level
- **Target:** Support level
- **Exit:** Trail SL or take profit at target
